Our Vision

CodelithLabs is a student-led coding organization at the Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar (Batch 2025–2028). Founded by Prasanta Ray, our team is committed to learning, growing, and building innovative projects together. Our mission is to improve every day, collaborate effectively, and become world-class developers.

Key Principles & Values

  • Teamwork: We believe in learning and building together.
  • Continuous Learning: Every day is an opportunity to improve.
  • Innovation: Creating solutions that matter.
  • Persistence: We embrace challenges and grow through them.
  • Integrity: Ethical practices in coding and collaboration.
